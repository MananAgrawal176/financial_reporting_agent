Given the **moderate upside** in analyst targets and the **balance of growth versus cash‑flow risk**, a **BUY** recommendation with **medium conviction** is appropriate. Investors should monitor debt‑service metrics and cash‑flow trends, and consider adding on dips if the price stays above the 50‑day SMA and volume improves.
