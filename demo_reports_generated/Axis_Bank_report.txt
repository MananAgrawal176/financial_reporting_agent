## Investment Brief: AXISBANK - Axis Bank Limited  
**Date:** 11 July 2026  
**Recommendation:** BUY  
**Conviction:** MEDIUM  

### The Bull Case  
- Axis Bank is delivering robust profitability with a **35.4% net profit margin** and **13.2% ROE**, well above many Indian peers.  
- Recent quarterly results highlighted **15% YoY deposit growth** and **14% YoY loan growth**, underpinning a solid earnings outlook and a **28% QoQ PAT rise**.

Given the solid fundamentals and favorable market narrative, the stock merits a **BUY** recommendation with **medium conviction**—appropriate for investors seeking growth exposure in Indian banking while monitoring top‑line trends and volume cues for entry timing.
